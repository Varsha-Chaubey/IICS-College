About Us

Our Goals & Core Values

IICS College have a shining stars and a mighty books as the symbols of speed and honesty. The complete idea reflects to the pace of growth with the pool of knowledge and tallents. The IICS College define the character of the college that is the student learning under one umbrella. To make our students success achievers by means of an ever growing standard of excellence in technical education is our vision. However, we intend to instill in them a deep understanding of human values and national ethics simultaneously.
IICS College takes a prismatic view of technical education. We hold that knowledge must reflect from the personality of our teachers to the students. But our broad vision also entails that humanity must also radiate from their faces.
